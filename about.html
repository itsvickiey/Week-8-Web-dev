Our Story: Growing Green Thumbs

From Backyard Hobby to Boutique Shop

Green Thumb was founded in 2020 by plant enthusiast Alex J. out of a tiny apartment balcony. Frustrated by generic, unhealthy plants found in big box stores, Alex set out to create a local resource for **high-quality, ethically-sourced, and rare indoor greenery**.

Today, we've grown into a community hub where plant novices and experts alike can find their perfect botanical companion, along with the knowledge needed to help it thrive.
